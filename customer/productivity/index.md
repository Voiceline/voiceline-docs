# Productivity Platform

The rep-facing layer of VoiceLine — voice-first workflows that replace manual CRM entry in the field.

## Overview

The Productivity Platform is what reps interact with every day. The assistant listens as reps speak and automatically creates structured CRM records. Reps document visits, log calls, create tasks, and capture leads in a fraction of the time — without opening the CRM.

## In this section

## How it works

1. Rep opens VoiceLine before or after a customer visit
2. Speaks naturally about what happened — the assistant extracts structured data
3. Rep reviews and submits — records sync to the CRM automatically
4. Manager sees the activity in Analytics the same day

No typing. No CRM login for reps. No data entry backlog.
