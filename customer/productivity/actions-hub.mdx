A web-based dashboard for reviewing, editing, and submitting CRM actions generated from your VoiceLine recordings.

## Overview

When you record a VoiceLine — whether it's a visit debrief, a call log, or a quick voice memo — the assistant generates one or more CRM actions from it. The Actions Hub gives you a single place in the web app to see all of those actions, review what the assistant produced, make edits, and submit them to your CRM.

This is especially useful when you want to catch up on multiple recordings at once, review actions on a larger screen, or handle actions that need manual input before they can sync.

<Note>The Actions Hub is currently in **Beta** and available to workspaces with the feature enabled. You can find it in the sidebar under **Beta Features → Actions**. If you don't see it, contact your workspace admin.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine web app. In the sidebar, look for the **Beta Features** section and select **Actions**. A badge on the menu item shows how many open actions are waiting for your review.

## Navigating the Actions Hub

The Actions Hub organises your actions into three tabs:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|---------------|
| **Open** | Actions that need your attention — review, edit, or submit them |
| **Scheduled** | Actions with a future date, such as [reminders](/customer/productivity/workflows/reminder) |
| **Done** | Actions that have been submitted and synced to your CRM |

Within each tab, items are grouped by date — **This week** and **Older** — so you can quickly find recent activity.

## Reviewing and submitting actions

Select any recording in the list to open the detail view. You'll see:

- The original VoiceLine recording and its transcript
- Each CRM action the assistant generated (visit reports, tasks, call logs, and other [workflows](/customer/productivity/workflows/index))
- The status of each action — whether it's ready to send, needs more information, or has already synced

From the detail view you can:

- **Edit** any action's fields before submitting
- **Submit** one or all actions to your CRM
- **Delete** actions you don't need
- **Reschedule** reminders to a different date
- **Create** additional actions from the same recording

If an action is missing required fields, you'll see a warning indicator. Fill in the missing information before submitting.

## FAQ

**Why don't I see Actions in my sidebar?**
The Actions Hub is in Beta and requires the feature to be enabled for your workspace. Contact your workspace admin if you'd like access.

**Can I still use the mobile app to review actions?**
Yes. The Actions Hub in the web app is an additional way to manage your actions — it doesn't replace the mobile experience.

**What happens if I edit an action in the web app and the mobile app at the same time?**
The most recently saved version takes effect. Avoid editing the same action in both places simultaneously.
