Document a customer visit in under 5 minutes — by talking, not typing.

## Overview

A visit report is the primary record of a field interaction. It captures what happened, who was present, and what comes next. VoiceLine lets you create one by speaking naturally immediately after leaving the customer — while everything is still fresh.

You open the assistant, select **Visit Report**, and start talking. The AI listens, extracts structured data from your words, and populates the report fields automatically. You review the output, make any edits, and submit — your CRM is updated without a single manual entry.

<Note>Available with **[Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals)**.</Note>

The exact fields in your visit report depend on how your workspace is configured. Typical sections include visit title, location, contacts present, a structured summary, and follow-up actions.

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap the **+** button → select **Visit Report**.

The workflow is also triggered automatically after a visit recording ends, as part of the post-meeting debriefing flow.

## Creating a visit report

1. **Start recording** — tap Visit Report and speak naturally about your visit. Cover who you met, what was discussed, any decisions made, and what happens next.
2. **Review the draft** — VoiceLine processes your recording and pre-fills all report fields. Review the output and correct anything the AI misunderstood.
3. **Submit** — the report syncs to your CRM instantly.

**Tips for better reports:**
- Record immediately after the visit while details are fresh
- Mention customer names, products discussed, and any commitments made — the AI will extract them
- You don't need to follow any structure; speak as you would on a phone call

## What syncs to your CRM

The completed visit report syncs to your CRM as a visit activity record. All fields are written to the CRM object your workspace is configured for (e.g. Activity, Visit, Appointment, depending on your CRM).

Your workspace admin controls which fields are mapped and what the AI extracts. Common mappings include:

| Report field | CRM field (example) |
|---|---|
| Title | Activity subject |
| Date & location | Visit date, location |
| Contacts present | Related contacts |
| Summary | Description / notes |
| Follow-up actions | Tasks (created separately) |

## FAQ

**How long should my recording be?**
There is no minimum or maximum. Most reps speak for 1–3 minutes. The AI handles any length.

**Can I edit the report before submitting?**
Yes. Always review the draft before submitting. You can edit any field directly in the app.

**What if I recorded in a different language?**
VoiceLine supports multilingual recording. The output language is set at the workspace level.

**Does the report sync immediately?**
Yes. Once you submit, the record appears in your CRM within seconds.

**Can I create a visit report without a recording?**
You can fill report fields manually if needed, but the voice-first flow is the intended path.
