Complete a structured customer survey — by speaking, not form-filling.

## Overview

The Survey workflow guides you through a predefined set of questions during or after a customer visit. Instead of typing answers into a form or checklist, you speak — the AI listens, extracts structured responses for each question, and submits them to your CRM.

This replaces the current pattern of forgetting to complete surveys, rushing through them inaccurately at the end of the week, or spending 30–60 minutes per visit on data entry for complex questionnaires.

Surveys in VoiceLine are created and managed by your workspace admin. They are linked to visit types, customer segments, or campaigns — the right survey appears automatically based on the visit context.

<Note>Will be available with the **[Marketing Surveys](/commercial/modules#marketing-surveys)** module.</Note>

## Planned capabilities

- **AI-guided questions** — the assistant asks predefined questions and records your verbal answers
- **Structured output** — free-form voice is mapped to specific field types: text, dropdowns, numeric, yes/no, tables
- **Context-aware triggers** — survey appears automatically based on visit type, customer segment, or rep role
- **Works during or after visits** — conversational mode (during the visit) or debriefing mode (immediately after)
- **Mandatory field enforcement** — the AI prompts for any required answers not yet captured

## What admins configure

- Question sets (up to hundreds of data points per survey depending on complexity)
- Trigger rules (which customer segment or visit type activates which survey)
- Field mappings to CRM objects

## FAQ

**How is this different from Playbooks?**
[Playbooks](/customer/coaching/playbooks/index) are qualitative coaching prompts — open-ended questions that surface during recordings to improve rep behavior. Surveys are structured data collection tools with specific field outputs that feed CRM data and analytics. Different purpose, different output.
