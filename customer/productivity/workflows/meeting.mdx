## Overview

The Meeting workflow creates a formal meeting record — subject, time, attendees, and agenda — from a short voice description. It syncs to your CRM and sends a calendar invite to the attendees.

This is designed for meetings agreed during a visit or call: the customer says "let's meet again in two weeks" and you capture it immediately, before the commitment slips.

<Note>Available with **[Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals)**.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap **+** → select **Meeting**.

## Creating a meeting

1. **Open Meeting** from the + menu.
2. **Speak the details** — describe the meeting: who it's with, when, where, and what it's about. Example: *"Schedule a follow-up meeting with Stefan and Lisa from Böllhoff, next Tuesday at 2pm at their office in Bielefeld, to review the proposal."*
3. **Review the draft** — the AI populates: subject, date/time, location, attendees, and description.
4. **Submit** — the meeting is created in your CRM and calendar invites are sent to the attendees listed.

## What syncs to your CRM

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Subject | AI-generated from voice |
| Date & time | Extracted from your recording |
| Location | Extracted from your recording |
| Attendees | Matched to CRM contacts or entered by name |
| Description | Purpose/agenda from your recording |

## FAQ

**Does the customer receive a calendar invite?**
Yes. Attendees listed in the meeting receive a calendar invite from you — just as if you had created it in Outlook or Google Calendar directly.

**What if I don't know the exact time yet?**
You can create the meeting without a fixed time and update it later. Mention "to be confirmed" in your recording and the time field will remain open for editing.

**Is this different from a Reminder?**
Yes. A [Reminder](/customer/productivity/workflows/reminder) is a personal note-to-self — it doesn't create CRM records or send invites. A Meeting creates a formal record in the CRM and notifies attendees.

**Can I create recurring meetings?**
Recurring meetings are not supported in the current version.
