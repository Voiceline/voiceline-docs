Capture a customer phone call while you're still in the car.

## Overview

A call log records a customer phone interaction — the topic, outcome, and any follow-up actions. Because phone calls happen while reps are driving, typing is not an option. VoiceLine lets you dictate the call summary by voice, immediately after hanging up, and have it land in your CRM automatically.

The experience is identical to a visit report: you speak, the AI structures your input, you review, and you submit.

<Note>Available with **[Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals)**.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap **+** → select **Call Log**.

## Creating a call log

1. **After hanging up**, open VoiceLine and tap Call Log.
2. **Speak naturally** — describe the call: who you spoke with, what was discussed, and any next steps.
3. **Review the draft** — the AI pre-fills the log fields from your recording.
4. **Submit** — the call is logged in your CRM.

**Capture while driving safely:**
- The app is designed for quick voice input — you don't need to look at the screen to record
- Even a 30-second summary is better than nothing
- You can also use the call log from a parked position between meetings

## What syncs to your CRM

A call log syncs to your CRM as a phone activity record (equivalent to a visit report, different activity type). Fields vary by CRM configuration but typically include:

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Title | Auto-generated from call content |
| Contact / Account | Linked from CRM search |
| Call summary | AI-structured from your voice |
| Follow-up actions | Extracted and listed |

## FAQ

**Is call log different from visit report?**
The input method is the same (voice recording). The output is the same structure. The difference is the activity type that gets written to the CRM — phone call vs. in-person visit. Your CRM may show them in different views.

**What if I can't record immediately after the call?**
Record as soon as it's safe to do so. The longer you wait, the more detail you lose. Even a brief recap right after hanging up is enough for the AI to generate a useful log.

**Can I log calls for contacts not yet in my CRM?**
You can record the call log and create the contact separately. If you know you're calling a new contact, consider creating the contact first — see [Contact](/customer/productivity/workflows/contact).
