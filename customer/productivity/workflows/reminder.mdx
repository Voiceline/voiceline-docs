Capture a thought while driving and have it show up in your calendar at the right time.

## Overview

A reminder in VoiceLine is a personal, time-based note-to-self. You speak what you need to remember and when — VoiceLine creates a calendar entry in your email client (Outlook, Gmail) that surfaces at the right moment.

Reminders are the lightest-weight workflow. No CRM sync, no assignee, no form to fill out. Just capture the thought before you forget it.

<Note>Available with **[Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals)**.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap **+** → select **Reminder**.

The assistant will only create a reminder if you explicitly ask for one. It won't auto-generate reminders from visit recordings.

## Creating a reminder

1. **Open Reminder** from the + menu — or tell the assistant directly: *"Set a reminder..."*
2. **Speak the reminder** — include what you need to remember and when. Example: *"Remind me on Friday at 9am to call back Jenny at Lighting Design Alliance."*
3. **Review the draft** — the AI extracts: title, date, and any additional notes.
4. **Submit** — a calendar event is created in your email client for the specified time.

The reminder appears as a calendar meeting in your inbox, giving you a notification at the set time.

## FAQ

**Where does the reminder appear?**
Reminders are created as calendar events in your connected email client (Outlook, Gmail, etc.). You'll receive a notification at the specified time, just like any other calendar event.

**Does it sync to the CRM?**
No. Reminders are personal and don't create CRM records. For a follow-up action that should be visible to your manager or inside sales, use a [Task](/customer/productivity/workflows/task) instead.

**Can I set a reminder from a visit report recording?**
Not automatically. To create a reminder after a visit, tap + and select Reminder as a separate workflow. You can also mention it during the visit recording and the assistant will prompt you to create it as a separate action.

**What if I don't specify an exact time?**
If you only say a date (e.g., "remind me next Monday"), the AI will create the calendar event for that day. Specify a time if you need a precise notification.
