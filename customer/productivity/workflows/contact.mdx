Create a new person in your CRM or update an existing one — by voice or business card scan.

## Overview

A contact in VoiceLine is a person record linked to an account in your CRM. You can create a new contact when you meet someone who isn't in the system yet, or update an existing contact when their details change.

Business cards are the primary source: field reps collect cards constantly but almost never enter them into the CRM manually — it's too slow and too easy to put off. VoiceLine's contact workflow makes it fast enough to do on the spot. The same applies to updates — when a contact changes role, gets a new phone number, or moves to a different office, you can update their record immediately instead of waiting until you're back at your desk.

Two input methods:

- **Voice** — describe the contact by speaking
- **Business card scan** — photograph the card; the AI extracts all text

<Note>Available with the **[Customer Data](/commercial/modules#customer-data)** module.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap **+** → select **Contact**.

## Creating a contact

### By voice

1. **Open Contact** from the + menu.
2. **Speak the details** — name, title, company, phone, email. Example: *"New contact: Anna Keller, Head of Procurement at Braun AG, her mobile is 0172-44556677."*
3. **Review the draft** — AI populates all extracted fields.
4. **Handle deduplication** — if a contact with the same name at the same account already exists, you can select the existing record. Some flows trigger follow-up actions (e.g. matching a contact to an account may prompt an opportunity).
5. **Submit** — contact is created in your CRM, linked to the relevant account.

### By business card

1. **Photograph the card** — front and back for best results. The AI reads all text.
2. **Review the extracted data** — name, title, company, phone, email, address.
3. **Handle deduplication** — same flow as above.
4. **Add a voice recording** (optional) — speak context about this person before submitting.
5. **Submit**.

The contact record is saved locally (on your device) in addition to syncing to the CRM.

## What syncs to your CRM

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| First name, last name | Extracted from voice or card |
| Title / role | Extracted from voice or card |
| Phone, email | Extracted from voice or card |
| Linked account | Selected from CRM or created alongside |
| Notes | From your voice recording |

## Updating a contact

Use this when a contact's details have changed — new phone number, different job title, updated email, or a move to a new address.

1. From a visit report or account context, select an existing contact to update — or record a voiceline that references the contact by name.
2. **Speak what changed** — *"Update Anna Keller's title to VP Procurement, new email is anna.keller@braun-group.com."*
3. **Review the changes** — the AI highlights which fields differ from the current CRM record. A **"X field(s) changed"** banner shows how many fields were updated.
4. **Check the details** — tap **Details** to see a side-by-side comparison of each changed field (current value vs. previous CRM value).
5. **Revert if needed** — if the AI extracted something incorrectly, revert individual fields or tap **Revert all** to restore every field to its original CRM value.
6. **Submit** — the CRM record is patched with only the changed fields.

You can also scan a business card during the update flow to populate fields automatically — useful when a contact hands you a new card with updated details.

## FAQ

**What if the person already exists in the CRM?**
When creating a contact, the deduplication step shows you existing contacts with similar names at the same company. Select the existing record to avoid duplicates — or choose to update it instead.

**Can I add context after scanning a business card?**
Yes. After reviewing the extracted card data, you can tap "Add recording" to speak additional context — relationship notes, what was discussed, next steps — before submitting.

**What if the contact's company doesn't exist in my CRM yet?**
Create the account first (see [Account](/customer/productivity/workflows/account)), then create the contact linked to it. Or mention the company in your voice recording and the AI will prompt you to link or create the account.

**Is the contact saved if I don't have a CRM connection?**
The contact data is saved locally on your device regardless of CRM sync status. It will sync when connectivity is restored.

**Can I undo a single field change before submitting?**
Yes. In the update review screen, tap **Details** to see all changed fields. Each field has a **Revert** button that restores the original CRM value. You can also tap **Revert all** to undo every change at once.

**For trade fair use — is this part of Fair Mode?**
Lead creation is the primary flow for trade fair contacts (not Contact). See [Lead](/customer/productivity/workflows/lead) and [Industry Fair Mode](/customer/productivity/assistant#fair-mode) for the full fair workflow.
