Create a new customer account in your CRM or update an existing one — by voice, on the spot.

## Overview

An account in VoiceLine is a company record in your CRM. You can create a new account when you discover a potential customer who doesn't yet exist in the system, or update an existing account when company details change.

Without this workflow, reps who meet new companies have no way to document the interaction until they return to a desk — and by then, context is lost. VoiceLine lets you create or update the account record on the spot, so subsequent visit reports, leads, and opportunities can be linked to accurate data immediately.

<Note>Available with the **[Customer Data](/commercial/modules#customer-data)** module.</Note>

## Where to find it

Open the VoiceLine mobile app → tap **+** → select **Account**.

## Creating an account

1. **Open Account** from the + menu.
2. **Speak the account details** — company name, address, industry, size, and any relevant context. Example: *"New account: Schmidt Hydraulik GmbH, based in Stuttgart, mechanical engineering, about 80 employees, they're currently using a competitor for our product category."*
3. **Review the draft** — the AI populates company name, address, industry classification, and notes.
4. **Handle deduplication** — VoiceLine searches your CRM for existing accounts with similar names or addresses. If a potential match is found, you can select the existing record instead of creating a duplicate.
5. **Submit** — the account is created in your CRM.

## What syncs to your CRM

The standard account generator creates the following fields:

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Company name | From voice |
| Address | From voice (street, city, country) |
| Account type / role | Prospect, Customer, or other — configurable |
| Industry | From voice or CRM picklist |
| Notes | Context from your recording |

CRM-specific fields (e.g. SAP C4C business partner role, Salesforce account type) are configured per workspace.

## Updating an existing account

When an account's details change — new phone number, updated address, different status — you can update the existing CRM record by voice instead of editing it manually.

1. **Open Account Update** from the + menu (your workspace may label this differently).
2. **Speak the changes** — mention the account and what changed. Example: *"Update Schmidt Hydraulik — they've moved to Karlsruhe, new address is Industriestr. 12, 76131 Karlsruhe."*
3. **Review the draft** — VoiceLine matches the account from your CRM and highlights any fields that differ from the current record. Changed fields appear with a blue border so you can spot them at a glance.
4. **Review updated fields** — tap **Details** to open the Updated Fields panel. This shows each changed field side by side: the new value and the previous value from your CRM.
5. **Revert if needed** — if any update is incorrect, tap **Revert** next to that field to restore the previous value. You can also tap **Revert all** to discard all changes at once.
6. **Submit** — the account record is updated in your CRM.

### Reviewing updated fields

The Updated Fields panel gives you full control before submitting. For each field that changed, you see:

- **Current** — the new value extracted from your voice
- **Previous** — the value currently stored in your CRM

This lets you confirm that the AI correctly interpreted your update and catch any mistakes before they reach the CRM. If you revert a field, it returns to the previous CRM value. Tap **Done** to close the panel and return to the form.

## FAQ

**What if the company already exists in the CRM?**
When creating an account, the deduplication step will show you the match. Select the existing account to avoid creating a duplicate — you can proceed to log a visit, create a contact, or update the existing record instead.

**Do I need to fill in the full address?**
The AI extracts whatever address information you provide. A city and country is enough for most CRM configurations, though some workspaces require full street addresses.

**Who can create accounts?**
Account creation is available to all reps. Your workspace admin may configure approval workflows or validation rules — newly created accounts may appear as "pending" until reviewed, depending on your CRM setup.

**Should I create the account before or after my visit report?**
Before. If the account doesn't exist in the CRM, create it first so you can link your visit report to it.

**How do I know which fields were updated?**
Changed fields are highlighted with a blue border on the form. Tap **Details** to see a side-by-side comparison of every changed field with its current and previous value.

**Can I undo a specific field update without discarding everything?**
Yes. In the Updated Fields panel, tap **Revert** next to any individual field to restore just that field to its CRM value. Other changes remain intact.
