# Platform Overview

VoiceLine is the operating system for enterprise field teams — turning every customer visit into structured CRM data, market intelligence, and coaching insights automatically.

## Overview

Field sales reps spend 20–30% of their working time on manual CRM documentation — after hours, from memory, with poor data quality. VoiceLine eliminates this by letting reps speak naturally after a visit and converting that into structured records that sync directly to the CRM.

Beyond data entry, every recording becomes a source of business intelligence: which competitors came up, which products were discussed, what the customer's sentiment was — all extracted automatically, without reps filling in extra fields.

## The four platforms

VoiceLine is organized into four interconnected platforms:

### Productivity Platform
What reps use every day. The voice assistant captures visit reports, call logs, tasks, leads, opportunities, and more. Reps speak — VoiceLine structures, reviews, and syncs.

## How data flows

```
Rep speaks after a visit
        ↓
VoiceLine assistant processes recording
        ↓
Structured record: visit report, opportunity, task, lead, etc.
        ↓
Rep reviews and submits
        ↓
Record syncs to CRM                Analytics data extracted
        ↓                                    ↓
CRM updated in real time       Manager sees insights same day
```

No manual CRM entry. No data backlog. No information lost between the parking lot and the office.

## Key concepts

**Workspace** — A VoiceLine workspace represents your organization. One workspace connects to one CRM and contains all your users, data objects, and analytics.

**Assistant** — The voice interface reps interact with. Available as a mobile app (iOS and Android) and via phone call. Reps speak naturally — the assistant handles transcription, extraction, and CRM mapping.

**Workflows** — The individual record types reps create: visit reports, call logs, tasks, emails, reminders, meetings, opportunities, leads, accounts, contacts. Each workflow has a configurable schema of fields.

**Data objects** — The master records VoiceLine makes searchable: accounts, contacts, products, competitors, leads, opportunities. Synced from your CRM and extended by rep activity.

**Bundles** — VoiceLine is sold in feature bundles. Each bundle unlocks a set of capabilities. See [Bundles](../../commercial/index.md) for what's included in each.

## FAQ

**Does VoiceLine replace the CRM?**
No — VoiceLine connects to your existing CRM and enriches it. Reps continue using VoiceLine for capture; managers use both VoiceLine and the CRM for reporting and management.

**Is VoiceLine available offline?**
The mobile app supports recording in low-connectivity conditions. Recordings sync to the server when connectivity is restored.

**Which languages are supported?**
VoiceLine supports major European languages including German, English, French, Italian, Spanish, and Dutch. See [Language Settings](../platform/language-settings.md).

**How long does setup take?**
A typical implementation — CRM integration, user import, language configuration — takes 1–3 days. See the [Quick Start](./quick-start.md) for the setup sequence.
