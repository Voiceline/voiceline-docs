Open deals in the pipeline — created and updated by reps directly from the field.

## Overview

Opportunities represent potential sales deals linked to accounts. VoiceLine syncs existing opportunities from your CRM and enables reps to create new opportunities or update existing ones via voice — capturing stage, amount, close date, and products without touching the CRM.

## Where to find it

Opportunities sync from your CRM automatically. Reps access opportunities via conversational search when creating or updating a deal: say the account name or opportunity name, and VoiceLine surfaces the matching record.

## Configuration

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Opportunity name or description |
| **Account** | Linked CRM account |
| **Stage** | Pipeline stage (values match your CRM's stage list) |
| **Amount** | Deal value |
| **Close date** | Expected close date |
| **Owner** | Assigned sales rep |
| **Products** | Linked products from the catalog |

Stage values are pulled from your CRM's picklist — VoiceLine reflects your configured stages.

## Search behavior

Opportunities are searched by name and account. When a rep updates an opportunity, VoiceLine surfaces the open opportunities linked to the currently selected account first.

For CRMs with large product catalogs (e.g., SAP, Salesforce), opportunity creation may be a two-step flow: first create the opportunity, then add products separately. See [Workflows — Opportunity](/customer/productivity/workflows/opportunity).

## FAQ

**Can reps add products to an opportunity from the field?**
Yes — for supported CRMs. Products from your catalog are searchable by name or product number. Multiple products can be added to a single opportunity.

**Do opportunity stage updates sync back to the CRM in real time?**
Yes — stage and amount updates are pushed to the CRM immediately on submission.

**Are opportunities visible to all reps or only to the owner?**
Visibility follows your [Search Settings](/customer/platform/search-settings) permission configuration. Typically, reps see opportunities assigned to them or to their group.
