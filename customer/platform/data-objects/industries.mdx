Market segments and industry classifications used to filter analytics and segment field intelligence.

## Overview

Industries (also called market subsegments) are the classification categories VoiceLine uses to group accounts by market vertical. They enable analytics filtering by industry — letting managers see how field performance, competitive pressure, or product mix differs across sectors.

## Where to find it

Industries sync from your CRM as part of the account data. Accounts have an industry or market segment field in the CRM that carries over to VoiceLine. No direct industry management UI exists in VoiceLine.

## Configuration

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Industry or market segment name |
| **External ID** | CRM-assigned identifier |

Industries are defined in your CRM and reflect your existing segmentation model. VoiceLine uses whatever classification exists on the account record — NACE codes, custom sector lists, or CRM picklist values.

## Search behavior

Industries are not searched directly by reps. They are used as **filters** in analytics views — managers can filter Competitor Analytics, Visit Analytics, and Team Activity by industry to segment results.

## FAQ

**Can I add custom industry classifications?**
Industries are synced from your CRM account records. To add or modify industries, update the classification in your CRM — changes propagate to VoiceLine at the next sync.

**What if accounts don't have an industry field in the CRM?**
Industry-based filtering in analytics will not be available until accounts have industry values populated in the CRM. Contact your VoiceLine account manager to explore alternative segmentation options.

**Are industries used in playbook distribution?**
Yes — playbooks can be distributed by industry segment. See [Playbooks](/customer/coaching/playbooks/index) for configuration details.
