Your product and service catalog — searchable by reps when logging offers, opportunities, or cross-sell recommendations.

## Overview

Products are your company's sellable items indexed in VoiceLine. Reps reference products when creating opportunities, logging offers in visit reports, or capturing what a customer discussed or ordered. Products can be synced from your CRM's product catalog or uploaded manually.

## Where to configure it

Go to **Workspace Settings** → **Product Recognition**.

From here, administrators can:
- Upload a product catalog manually (CSV/Excel)
- Connect to a CRM product catalog (where supported by your integration)
- Edit product names, IDs, and categories

## Configuration

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Product name as reps reference it |
| **Product ID** | Internal or CRM-assigned identifier |
| **Brand** | Brand or product line |
| **Category** | Product category for filtering |

Product names should reflect how reps actually refer to products in conversation — VoiceLine uses these names for conversational recognition. If reps commonly use abbreviations or trade names, add them as alternative names.

## Search behavior

Reps search products by name during opportunity creation or offer logging. Phonetic matching is active — minor pronunciation variations resolve correctly. The search is scoped to the product catalog for that workspace.

For large catalogs (tens of thousands of SKUs), VoiceLine performs an extended search — the assistant prompts the rep to narrow down by product name, number, or category.

## FAQ

**How large can the product catalog be?**
VoiceLine supports catalogs of up to hundreds of thousands of products. Large catalogs may require more specific search queries to narrow results.

**Can reps add products during a visit report?**
Products are primarily referenced in opportunity and offer contexts. Mention a product by name during a visit report and VoiceLine extracts it for cross-selling analytics.

**How often does the product catalog sync from the CRM?**
Sync frequency depends on the CRM integration. Manual uploads are applied immediately. For automated syncs, products update on the scheduled refresh cycle.
