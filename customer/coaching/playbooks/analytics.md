# Playbook Analytics

See how often your team is using playbooks and what they're capturing in each question.

## Overview

Playbook Analytics closes the feedback loop. You've defined the questions — now you can see whether they're being used, how completely, and what your reps are actually saying in response.

Two views give you different levels of detail: **Performance** shows usage and completion rates across your team, so you can spot which reps are engaging with playbooks and which aren't. **Answers** surfaces the actual content — the specific responses to each question, across all reps and visits — letting you read the market signal your playbooks are collecting.

> [!NOTE]
> Playbook Analytics is available with the **[Field Sales Fundamentals](../../../commercial/field-sales-fundamentals.md)** bundle (single playbook) and **[Sales Coaching Pro](../../../commercial/modules.md#sales-coaching-pro)** (multiple playbooks).

## Where to find it

Open the web app → **Playbook** in the sidebar → **Analytics**.

## Performance tab

The Performance tab shows how thoroughly your team is working through their playbooks.

For each team member, you'll see:

- **Completed** — the rep addressed all questions in the playbook
- **Partially completed** — the rep addressed some but not all questions
- **Not used** — the playbook was present during recording but not engaged with

A **coverage trend chart** shows how playbook usage is changing over time — useful for spotting whether a newly introduced playbook is gaining traction.

Use the playbook selector at the top to filter the view to a specific playbook, or compare across all active playbooks.

## Answers tab

The Answers tab lets you drill into the content of what reps are capturing.

Select a question to see the responses collected across all visits where that playbook was active. This is where the market intelligence lives: customer reactions to a new product, mentions of a specific competitor, recurring objections your team is hearing in the field.

## Filters

Both tabs can be filtered by:
- **Playbook** — focus on a specific playbook
- **Team member** — look at an individual rep's usage
- **Date range** — track trends over a specific period

## FAQ

**Why is a rep showing "not used" even though the playbook was active during their recording?**
"Not used" means the playbook was shown but the rep didn't address any of the questions during the recording. It does not mean the playbook wasn't visible to them.
