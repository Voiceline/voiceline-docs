Tools for sales managers to understand field performance, coach reps, and drive consistent execution.

The Coaching Platform gives managers visibility into what their team is actually doing in the field — not just outcomes, but activity quality. Playbooks standardize what reps should capture during visits. Visit Analytics scores the quality of each interaction. Team Activity tracks who's doing what and at what volume.

## In this section

| Section | What it covers |
|---------|---------------|
| [Playbooks](/customer/coaching/playbooks/index) | Create question sets that guide reps through visits and capture structured market intelligence |
| [Team Activity](/customer/coaching/team-activity) | Activity dashboards — action counts, daily trends, per-rep profiles, visit feed |
| [Visit Analytics](/customer/coaching/visit-analytics) | Visit quality scoring (richness), live visit feed, and performance benchmarks |

## Who uses it

| Role | What they use |
|------|--------------|
| **Sales manager** | Team Activity to track rep output; Visit Analytics to spot quality gaps; Playbooks to standardize execution |
| **Sales leadership** | Team Activity aggregated across groups; Visit Analytics trends by territory |
| **Rep** | Playbook questions during recordings; personal activity profile |

## How playbooks and analytics connect

Playbooks define what reps should capture → reps follow playbooks during recordings → Visit Analytics scores whether they did → managers use those scores to coach the right reps on the right topics.
