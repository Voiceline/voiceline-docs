The Lead workflow lets reps create and qualify new leads by voice — capturing contact details and initial qualification data immediately after an interaction.

## What it captures

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| First name | Text | Lead's first name |
| Last name | Text | Lead's last name |
| Company | Text | Lead's company or organisation |
| Job title | Text | Lead's role |
| Phone | Text | Contact phone number |
| Email | Text | Contact email address |
| Lead source | Picklist | How the lead was acquired (e.g. trade fair, referral, cold call) |
| Lead status | Picklist | Initial qualification status |
| Notes | Long text | Initial context, interest areas, next steps |

## Deduplication

Before creating a new lead, VoiceLine checks for existing records in the CRM with matching name and company. If a potential duplicate is detected, the rep is shown the existing record and prompted to confirm whether to create a new record or update the existing one.

See [CRM Data Quality](/customer/productivity/customer-data-quality) for full deduplication documentation.

## What can be configured

- **Lead source values** — customise to match your acquisition channels
- **Lead status values** — align with your qualification framework
- **Required fields** — define which fields must be populated (e.g. phone or email required)
- **Deduplication threshold** — configure how strict the duplicate detection is
- **Lead routing** — configure which rep or team a lead is assigned to on creation

## What the standard does not include

- Automatic lead scoring
- Lead conversion to contact/account (done manually in CRM after creation)
- Territory-based automatic assignment (requires CRM configuration)

## FAQ

**What happens if the lead already exists in the CRM?**
VoiceLine detects the potential duplicate and shows the rep the existing record. The rep can choose to update the existing record instead of creating a new one.

**Can we capture leads for contacts who are already in the CRM?**
If the person already exists as a Contact or Account, the standard workflow routes to the Contact or Account creation flow instead. Your SA will configure the overlap logic during implementation.
