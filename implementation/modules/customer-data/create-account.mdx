The Account workflow lets reps update account information and add notes by voice — keeping account records current without manual CRM entry.

## What it captures

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Account | Lookup | The account being updated |
| Account notes | Long text | General account context, relationship notes |
| Account status | Picklist | e.g. active, at-risk, expansion opportunity |
| Key contacts | Lookup (multi) | Contacts relevant to the account |
| Next visit date | Date | When the rep plans to visit next |
| Account health | AI-scored | Overall sentiment and relationship quality assessment |

## Deduplication

Account deduplication checks for existing records with matching name and address before any new account creation. VoiceLine does not create new account records by default — reps select from existing CRM accounts. New account creation is a separate, explicit action.

## What can be configured

- **Account status values** — customise to reflect your account health taxonomy
- **Custom account fields** — add fields specific to your business (e.g. segment, region, customer tier)
- **Next visit date logic** — configure default offset and reminder behaviour
- **Account lookup scope** — filter which accounts are visible to each rep (by territory, team, or assignment)

## What the standard does not include

- New account creation by voice (reps update existing accounts; new account creation is done via the CRM or a separate flow)
- Account hierarchy mapping (parent/subsidiary relationships managed in CRM)

## FAQ

**Can reps create new accounts by voice?**
New account creation by voice is not part of the standard Account workflow — it requires a deliberate configuration decision due to the risk of creating duplicate accounts. If your process requires voice-based account creation, discuss this with your SA during implementation scoping.

**How are accounts scoped per rep?**
Account visibility follows your CRM's existing assignment and territory logic. VoiceLine reads the accounts assigned to each rep's CRM user and surfaces only those accounts in the lookup.
