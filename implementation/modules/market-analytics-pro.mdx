The Market Analytics Pro module extends VoiceLine's built-in analytics with custom tracking. You define the competitors, products, and market signals you want to track — VoiceLine detects and extracts these automatically from every voice recording across your team.

For a full description of what the module includes commercially, see [Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro).

## What it activates

| Capability | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Custom competitor trackers | Define specific competitors to track mentions and context |
| Custom product trackers | Track your own and competitors' products across recordings |
| Industry signal trackers | Track market-specific topics, terminology, or signals |
| Analytics dashboards | Aggregated trend data across team, time period, and customer segment |

FSF includes standard competitor, product, and industry detection. Market Analytics Pro adds custom tracker configuration and the advanced analytics surface.

## Standard implementation

Market Analytics Pro adds no new workflow components. The tracking runs in the background on all recordings — there is nothing for reps to do differently.

Implementation involves configuring the tracker catalogue in your workspace:

| Tracker type | What to provide |
|-------------|----------------|
| Competitors | List of competitor names and known aliases |
| Products | Your product names and the competitors' product names to track |
| Industry signals | Keywords, phrases, or topic clusters to detect |

## Required scoping decisions

**Tracker catalogue:** Provide the list of competitors, products, and signals to track. This is the primary input for configuration.

**Taxonomy alignment:** Confirm whether the tracker categories should align with your existing CRM competitor and product picklists, or use a separate classification.

**Dashboard access:** Confirm which team members (managers, product team, marketing) should have access to the analytics dashboards.

## Dependencies

Market Analytics Pro has no module dependencies. It can be activated as the first module after FSF.
