Contact sync depends on account sync being complete — visibility and search are account-scoped, so confirm account configuration before starting here. For how contacts work in VoiceLine, see [Contacts](/customer/platform/data-objects/contacts).

By the end of this page your FDE will have confirmed with your CRM admin:

- The fields needed to reliably identify contacts within an account
- The sync filters to apply

## Contact identification

The goal is ensuring reps can reliably retrieve the correct contact during a workflow. For most teams this means first name + last name is sufficient within an account scope, but teams with common names in their contact data may need additional fields.

Confirm with your CRM admin which fields are consistently populated before enabling them as search criteria — sparsely populated fields are not reliable.

## Fields to sync

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| First name | Required | Primary search field |
| Last name | Required | Primary search field |
| Account link | Required | Account-scoped search and deduplication |
| CRM contact ID | Required | CRM record linkage |
| Email address | Optional | Extended search, deduplication |

## Sync filters

Filters control which contacts are synced from the CRM into VoiceLine. Contacts excluded by a filter are not visible to any rep.

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Active only | Exclude archived or inactive contacts |
| Linked to synced accounts only | Exclude contacts whose parent account is outside VoiceLine's account scope |

The account-scoped filter is recommended for all deployments — syncing contacts without a matching account serves no purpose and adds noise to lookup results.

## Permissions

Contact visibility is derived from account visibility — if a rep can see an account, they can see all contacts linked to that account. Contacts linked to accounts outside a rep's scope are not visible, regardless of contact ownership in the CRM. There is no separate contact-level permission model to configure.
