The Call Log captures phone calls with customers — reps speak a summary immediately after the call, and VoiceLine extracts the content and syncs it to the CRM.

## What it captures

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Call summary | Long text | AI-generated summary of the call content |
| Account | Lookup | The customer account called |
| Contact | Lookup | The contact person spoken to |
| Call date | Date | Date of the call (auto-populated) |
| Call direction | Picklist | Inbound or outbound |
| Call outcome | Picklist | e.g. reached, left voicemail, follow-up required |
| Next steps | Text | Action items mentioned during the call |
| Duration | Number | Call duration in minutes (if available from device) |

## CRM sync

| CRM | Target object | Sync trigger |
|-----|--------------|-------------|
| SAP C4C | Phone Call Activity | On submission |
| Salesforce | Task (Call type) | On submission |
| MS Dynamics | Phone Call Activity | On submission |
| HubSpot | Call Activity | On submission |

For field-level mappings per CRM, see the [Integrations](/integrations) section.

## Default schema

The fields listed above are the standard call log schema. The schema is configurable using the component library: fields can be added, removed, relabelled, or made required.

To add, remove, or change components, see the [Component Library](/implementation/workflow-customisation/component-library).

## Customising the schema

- **Call outcome values** — customise picklist to match your sales process
- **Call direction** — enable or disable the direction field depending on your CRM's requirements
- **Additional structured fields** — add fields relevant to phone-based sales (e.g. lead stage, product discussed)
- **Field labels** — rename to match CRM terminology

## Hard-coded fields

Hard-coded fields are FDE-configured values stamped on every call log submission — regardless of what the rep enters. They do not appear in the rep's UI.

| Example use case | Hard-coded field | Value |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Tag all VoiceLine records | Source | VoiceLine |
| Enforce activity subtype | Activity type | Phone Call |
| Stamp team or channel | Channel | Field Sales |

Your FDE configures hard-coded fields during workflow setup. Define the field name, the fixed value, and the CRM target field for each one.

## FAQ

**Can reps log a call while still on the phone?**
The standard workflow is designed for post-call documentation — reps speak immediately after hanging up, while the call is fresh. This produces better quality summaries than live transcription.

**Does VoiceLine record the actual phone call?**
No — VoiceLine captures a spoken summary after the call, not a recording of the call itself. The rep dictates what happened and VoiceLine extracts the structured content.

**What if we want to log calls from our office team as well?**
The Call Log workflow works for any phone-based interaction. For high-volume office or inside sales teams with different workflow needs, see the [Inside Sales](/commercial/modules/inside-sales) module.
