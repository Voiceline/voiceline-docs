VoiceLine uses product data to recognise product names and references in voice recordings. When a rep mentions a product by name, VoiceLine extracts it as structured data — linking it to the correct product record and surfacing it in visit report summaries, analytics, and CRM sync.

Without product data configured, VoiceLine cannot reliably identify products mentioned in recordings. This step is required for any workspace where product references in visit reports are meaningful.

## How product data is provided

Product data is provided as a structured upload to the VoiceLine workspace. Your SA will import the data during configuration — no technical integration is required.

The upload is a CSV or Excel file with the following required fields:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Product ID | Yes | Unique identifier — typically the product ID from your CRM or ERP |
| Product name | Yes | The primary name used in your CRM |
| Synonyms | No | Alternative names, abbreviations, or common spoken variants |
| Category | No | Product category or group — used for analytics grouping |

## What VoiceLine does with it

Once imported, VoiceLine uses the product data in two ways:

1. **Voice recognition**: Product names and synonyms are added to the AI vocabulary. This improves recognition accuracy for names that are unusual, abbreviated, or likely to be misheard.

2. **Structured extraction**: When a product name is detected in a recording, VoiceLine links the mention to the product record and extracts it as a structured field — available for CRM sync and analytics.

## Keeping product data current

Product data is not automatically synced from your CRM or ERP. Updates (new products, discontinued products, renamed products) must be re-imported.

For most customers, a periodic re-import (quarterly or at major product release cycles) is sufficient. If your product catalogue changes frequently, discuss a sync approach with your SA.

<Note>Product data is imported manually — there is no automatic sync from your CRM or ERP unless a custom sync is configured. All synonyms in the upload should be in the languages your reps record in. If your product catalogue changes frequently, discuss a sync cadence with your SA.</Note>
