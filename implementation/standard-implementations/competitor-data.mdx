VoiceLine detects competitor mentions in voice recordings and surfaces them in analytics. When a rep mentions a competitor during a visit or call, VoiceLine extracts the reference — capturing the competitor name, the context, and the account it was mentioned at.

FSF includes built-in competitor tracking for the most commonly encountered competitors. Custom competitor configuration is available as part of the [Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro) module.

## How competitor data is provided

Competitor data is provided as a structured upload. Your SA imports it during workspace setup.

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Competitor name | Yes | The primary name used in your market |
| Aliases | No | Alternative names, abbreviations, or informal names reps use when speaking |
| Competitor ID | No | Optional reference ID for CRM alignment |

**Aliases are important.** Reps rarely refer to competitors by their full formal name. If a competitor is commonly called by an abbreviation or shorthand (e.g. "SF" for Salesforce, "SAP" for SAP SE), include those as aliases to ensure recognition accuracy.

## What gets detected

Once competitor data is configured, VoiceLine detects competitor mentions across all recordings:

- **Visit reports** — competitor mentions in the visit summary or any text field
- **Call logs** — competitor mentions during outbound calls
- **Any workflow** — detection runs on the full transcript, not just named fields

Detected mentions are extracted with context — VoiceLine captures the sentence or phrase where the competitor was mentioned, not just the name.

## Where competitor data surfaces

| Surface | What you see |
|---------|-------------|
| Visit report | Competitor mentions highlighted in the summary |
| Analytics — Competitor tab | Mention frequency by competitor, time period, and account segment |
| Visit briefing | Prior competitor mentions at the account (if any) |

The Competitor Analytics tab in the product shows trend data across the team. See [Competitor Analytics](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) for the full description.

<Note>For competitor product-level tracking and advanced analytics, see the [Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro) module. Win/loss attribution can be configured — discuss with your SA if this is relevant to your setup.</Note>
