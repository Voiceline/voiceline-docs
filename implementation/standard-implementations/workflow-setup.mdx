FSF includes seven workflows that reps use to document their daily activity by voice. Three of them — Visit Report, Call Log, and Task — have configurable schemas that must be tailored to your CRM structure and sales process before go-live. The remaining four follow a fixed standard and require no FDE configuration.

By the end of this section your FDE will have confirmed with your project lead:

- The schema for each configurable workflow, including any fields added beyond the standard
- The hard-coded fields to stamp on each workflow's CRM record on submission

## Primary object: Account

Every workflow is anchored to a CRM account record. The rep selects the account once at the start of a recording — it applies to all workflows created in that session. Reps do not re-select the account per workflow.

This means Account is not part of any individual workflow schema. It is a session-level input, always present, always resolved before any workflow opens.

## FSF workflows

| Workflow | What it does | FDE configuration |
|---|---|---|
| [Visit Report](/implementation/standard-implementations/visit-report) | Documents customer visits by voice; syncs to CRM activity record | Schema, event selection, hard-coded fields |
| [Call Log](/implementation/standard-implementations/call-log) | Documents phone calls by voice; syncs to CRM call record | Schema, hard-coded fields |
| [Task](/implementation/standard-implementations/task) | Creates follow-up tasks by voice; syncs to CRM task object | Schema, hard-coded fields |
| Email | Drafts and sends emails to customers by voice | Standard — no configuration required |
| Meeting | Creates meetings in calendar and CRM by voice | Standard — no configuration required |
| Reminder | Sets personal push-notification reminders by voice | Standard — no configuration required |
| Visit Preparation | Surfaces a pre-visit briefing before each recording | Standard — no configuration required |

For how Email, Meeting, Reminder, and Visit Preparation work from the rep's perspective, see their respective pages in the product docs: [Email](/customer/productivity/workflows/email), [Meeting](/customer/productivity/workflows/meeting), [Reminder](/customer/productivity/workflows/reminder), [Visit Briefing](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing).
