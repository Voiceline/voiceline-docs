VoiceLine is built for enterprise field sales teams — which means it handles customer interaction data, CRM credentials, and business-critical workflows. Security is a foundational requirement, not an afterthought.

## Trust Centre

The authoritative source for VoiceLine's security documentation is our **Vanta-hosted Trust Centre**:

**[trust.voiceline.ai](https://trust.voiceline.ai)**

The Trust Centre contains:
- Security certifications and audit reports
- Penetration test reports
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) templates
- Sub-processor list
- Compliance status (SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR)
- Policies: access control, incident response, business continuity

If you need security documentation for a procurement review, start at the Trust Centre. Most questions are answered there.

## Common procurement questions

**Do you have a SOC 2 report?**
Yes. The current report is available in the Trust Centre. Contact your SA or CSM to request access under NDA if required.

**Can we sign a DPA before contract signature?**
Yes — DPA templates are available in the Trust Centre. Your SA can facilitate the signing process.

**Where is our data hosted?**
All VoiceLine customer data is hosted in AWS Frankfurt (eu-central-1). We do not use US-based data centres for customer data.

**Do you support SSO?**
Yes — SAML 2.0 SSO is supported on the Enterprise platform package. See [Security & Compliance](/customer/platform/security) for configuration details.

**Can VoiceLine employees access our CRM data?**
CRM credentials are encrypted at rest and are not accessible to VoiceLine employees. Voice recordings and transcription data are processed by our AI pipeline and are not reviewed by employees except in the context of contracted support cases (with customer consent).

**Do you conduct penetration tests?**
Yes — annual penetration tests are conducted by an independent third party. The most recent report is available in the Trust Centre.
