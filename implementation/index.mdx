Implementing VoiceLine is not an IT project. You don't need internal developers, a project manager, or months of preparation. Your Solutions Architect handles the configuration end to end — your job is to make a handful of decisions about your sales process.

We have implemented VoiceLine with hundreds of field sales teams across industries, CRM platforms, and company sizes. We know what works. We bring that to every engagement.

## What we bring to your implementation

**A proven standard.** Our workflows are designed from the ground up for field sales adoption. We don't start from a blank canvas — we start from what we know works, and adapt it to your setup.

**Hands-on configuration.** Your SA configures your workspace, integrates your CRM, sets up your workflows, and onboards your team. You review and approve, not build and maintain.

**A methodology, not a migration.** We don't replicate your existing CRM processes into VoiceLine. If those processes drove adoption, you wouldn't be looking for a new tool. We bring our model of how field sales documentation should work — built from years of working with leading organisations — and configure it to your specific environment.

**Experienced consultants.** If your sales process has quirks, edge cases, or requirements that don't fit our standard at first glance, your SA's job is to consult — to help you find the version of your process that works well in VoiceLine. Most of the time, that means simplifying something, not building something new.

## How an implementation unfolds

Every VoiceLine project follows the same proven sequence:

1. **[Field Sales Fundamentals goes live first](/implementation/our-approach/how-we-work)** — before any modules are activated. This is your foundation: core workflows live, CRM sync confirmed, your team documenting consistently.
2. **Steady state and adoption** — your team builds the habit. Your SA is with you.
3. **[Module expansion](/implementation/our-approach/how-we-work#expanding-with-modules)** — once FSF is solid, you add capabilities one at a time, each with its own consultation and rollout.

See [How We Work](/implementation/our-approach/how-we-work) for the full journey, including example timelines.
