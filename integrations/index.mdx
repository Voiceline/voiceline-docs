VoiceLine integrates with your CRM bidirectionally — pulling account and contact data so reps have context in the field, and writing structured activity records back after each interaction.

All integration configuration is handled at the workspace level by an administrator. Individual reps do not need to configure anything.

## Network requirements

Some CRM environments (particularly SAP and Aurea on-premise deployments) are accessible only via corporate VPN or a whitelisted IP range. Ensure VoiceLine's servers can reach your CRM's API endpoint before starting setup. Contact your IT team to confirm firewall rules if the connection test fails.

## FAQ

**Can VoiceLine connect to a CRM not listed here?**
Additional integrations are evaluated based on customer demand. Contact your account manager.

**Who manages the integration credentials?**
A workspace administrator stores credentials in VoiceLine's integration settings. Credentials are encrypted at rest. Reps do not need direct CRM access.

**What happens if the CRM connection drops?**
VoiceLine queues outbound records and retries delivery. Reps are not blocked from creating records — they sync once the connection is restored.
