## Sync overview

| Object | Direction | Trigger |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Accounts | Salesforce → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Contacts | Salesforce → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Leads | Salesforce → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Opportunities | Salesforce → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Campaigns | Salesforce → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |

| Call logs | VoiceLine → Salesforce Task (Call type) | On submission |

| New leads | VoiceLine → Salesforce Lead | On submission |
| New contacts | VoiceLine → Salesforce Contact | On submission |
| Opportunity updates | VoiceLine → Salesforce Opportunity | On submission |

## Sync cadence

| Direction | Trigger | Details |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Salesforce → VoiceLine | On rep login | Full refresh of assigned accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities |
| Salesforce → VoiceLine | Scheduled | Background refresh — interval configured during implementation (typically 4–6 hours) |
| VoiceLine → Salesforce | On submission | Immediate; queued and retried if Salesforce is unreachable |

## What VoiceLine does not sync from Salesforce

- Cases, service tickets
- Custom Salesforce objects not in the standard data model
- Salesforce reports or dashboards
- Email inbox content
- Chatter / internal Salesforce messages

<Note>Field mappings shown here are the standard defaults. Your SA may configure different mappings to match your Salesforce field names and picklist values during implementation.</Note>
