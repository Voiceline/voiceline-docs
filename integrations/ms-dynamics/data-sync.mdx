This page documents what data flows between VoiceLine and Microsoft Dynamics 365, in which direction, and on what schedule.

## Sync overview

| Object | Direction | Trigger |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Accounts | Dynamics → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Contacts | Dynamics → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Leads | Dynamics → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| Opportunities | Dynamics → VoiceLine | On login + scheduled refresh |
| CRM users | Dynamics → VoiceLine | On login |
| Visit reports | VoiceLine → Dynamics Appointment | On submission |
| Call logs | VoiceLine → Dynamics Phone Call | On submission |
| Tasks | VoiceLine → Dynamics Task | On submission |
| Meetings | VoiceLine → Dynamics Appointment | On submission |
| New leads | VoiceLine → Dynamics Lead | On submission |
| New contacts | VoiceLine → Dynamics Contact | On submission |
| New accounts | VoiceLine → Dynamics Account | On submission |
| Opportunity updates | VoiceLine → Dynamics Opportunity | On submission |

## Sync cadence

| Direction | Trigger | Details |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Dynamics → VoiceLine | On rep login | Full refresh of assigned accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities |
| Dynamics → VoiceLine | Scheduled | Background refresh — interval configured during implementation (typically 4–6 hours) |
| VoiceLine → Dynamics | On submission | Immediate; queued and retried if Dynamics is unreachable |

## What VoiceLine does not sync

- Dynamics Customer Service cases
- Power Platform flows or automation
- SharePoint or Teams content
- Email inbox content

<Note>Field-level mappings shown here are the standard defaults. Your SA may configure different mappings during implementation to match your Dynamics field names and picklist values. Deviations from the standard are documented during your implementation scoping.</Note>
