**500 reps · Platform Enterprise · All modules**

A large enterprise with VoiceLine deployed across the full field organisation. SSO required, workspace partitioning used to separate divisions or geographies. All modules active — reps document visits, create opportunities and leads, run surveys, create service cases, and capture fair interactions. Analytics and coaching used centrally by management and enablement teams.

<Note>Marketing Surveys and Service Cases are listed as Coming Soon. This scenario includes their module fees at projected pricing and models their credit contribution at expected usage levels.</Note>

## Credit usage

### Included baseline

500 reps × 500 credits = **250,000 credits / month** included in FSF.

### Assumed workflow frequency

Usage assumes high field activity across a mature deployment: approximately 18 field days per rep per month, high module adoption across all workflow types. Analytics insight extraction runs automatically from every visit at 3 insights per visit (range: 2–5).

## Sensitivity analysis: analytics insights per visit

Surveys (350 credits/rep/month) are now the single largest output driver, accounting for ~22% of total credit consumption. Analytics insights are the second-largest variable, accounting for ~13%.

| Insights / visit | Credits / rep / month | Monthly consumption | Annual overage | Annual credit cost |
|-----------------|-----------------------|---------------------|---------------|-------------------|
| 2 | 1,530 | 765,000 | ~515,000 overage/mo | ~€186,000 (31 Enterprise pkgs) |
| 3 (base) | 1,600 | 800,000 | ~550,000 overage/mo | ~€198,000 (33 Enterprise pkgs) |
| 5 | 1,740 | 870,000 | ~620,000 overage/mo | ~€228,000 (38 Enterprise pkgs) |

The insight range now drives a €36,000 spread (€252k–€288k/year). See open decision D2c in [Pricing Architecture](/commercial/pricing-architecture).
