**20 reps · Platform Professional · No modules**

A mid-sized field sales team deploying VoiceLine as a productivity and market intelligence tool. German and English, no SSO requirement, no add-on modules. Primary use case: documenting visits by voice and capturing market signals automatically.

## Credit usage

### Included baseline

20 reps × 500 credits = **10,000 credits / month** included in FSF.

### Assumed workflow frequency

Usage assumes moderate field activity: approximately 15 field days and 10 office days per rep per month. Analytics insight extraction runs automatically from every visit recording as part of FSF — estimated at 3 insights per visit (range: 2–5).

| Output | Credits each | Volume / rep / month | Credits / rep / month |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|----------------------|
| Visit briefing generated | 4 | 45 | 180 |
| Visit report created | 3 | 45 | 135 |
| Call log created | 3 | 60 | 180 |
| Task created | 1 | 20 | 20 |
| Email draft created | 1 | 10 | 10 |
| Reminder created | 1 | 100 | 100 |
| Analytics insight extracted | 1 | 135 (45 visits × 3 insights) | 135 |
| **Total** | | | **760** |

<Note>No modules active — opportunity creation, contact/account management workflows are not included in this scenario.</Note>
