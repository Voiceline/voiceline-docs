Worked examples of the VoiceLine pricing model applied to three representative customer profiles. Each scenario calculates the full monthly and annual cost across all four pricing layers — FSF, Platform, Modules, and Credits — using realistic usage assumptions.

## How to read these scenarios

Each page breaks down the pricing into:
- **Fixed monthly fees** — FSF per-seat charges, Platform flat fee, Module flat fees
- **Credit usage** — estimated monthly consumption by workflow output type and call minutes, compared against the included baseline, and the annual package cost for the overage
- **Total** — monthly and annual figures

Credit estimates are based on assumed workflow frequencies per rep per month. These assumptions are documented on each page and should be validated against actual customer usage patterns as data accumulates.

See [Credits](/commercial/credits) for the full credit weight schedule and package pricing.
