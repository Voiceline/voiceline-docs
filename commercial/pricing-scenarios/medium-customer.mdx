**80 reps · Platform Enterprise · Market Analytics Pro + Sales Coaching Pro + Pipeline Management**

A large field sales organisation with a mature VoiceLine rollout. SSO deployed, multi-country team with languages beyond German and English. Analytics and coaching modules active — management uses VoiceLine data in QBRs. Pipeline Management captures a high volume of opportunities from field conversations.

## Credit usage

### Included baseline

80 reps × 500 credits = **40,000 credits / month** included in FSF.

### Assumed workflow frequency

Usage assumes active field activity: approximately 15–18 field days per rep per month. Analytics insight extraction runs automatically from every visit — estimated at 3 insights per visit (range: 2–5). Pipeline Management drives 20 new opportunities per rep per month.

| Output | Credits each | Volume / rep / month | Credits / rep / month |

| Visit report created | 3 | 60 | 180 |

| Opportunity created | 4 | 20 | 80 |

| Task created | 1 | 40 | 40 |

### Call minutes

An estimated 40% of workflow submissions are made via the conversational phone assistant, at an average of 2 minutes per call. Each live minute consumes 5 credits.

| | Per rep / month |

| Estimated phone calls (40%) | 128 |
| Call credits (128 × 2 min × 5 credits) | **1,280** |
