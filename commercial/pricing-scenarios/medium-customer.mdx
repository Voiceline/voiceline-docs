## Credit usage

### Included baseline

80 reps × 500 credits = **40,000 credits / month** included in FSF.

### Assumed workflow frequency

Usage assumes active field activity: approximately 15–18 field days per rep per month. Analytics insight extraction runs automatically from every visit — estimated at 3 insights per visit (range: 2–5). Pipeline Management drives 20 new opportunities per rep per month.
