## Why it exists

Marketing survey data is only useful if it's complete, timely, and structured. In practice, field reps fill in surveys at the end of the week from memory, skip questions they can't remember the answers to, and spend significant time on data entry that adds no selling value. Response rates are low, data quality is poor, and the insights that drive campaign decisions are built on incomplete foundations.

Marketing Surveys replaces the form-filling workflow with a voice-guided survey experience. The assistant asks the configured questions, the rep speaks the answers, and the structured responses are extracted and synced to the CRM immediately — during or right after the visit. Walk-and-talk mode is designed specifically for store visits where reps move through a location rather than sitting in a meeting room.
