> **€600 / month**

Adds voice-guided survey completion and a walk-and-talk mode for store visits to Field Sales Fundamentals.

<Note>This module is in development. Contact your VoiceLine account manager for expected availability.</Note>

Marketing Surveys adds voice-guided survey completion to the FSF visit workflow. Surveys created by your marketing team are synced from the CRM and presented to reps as part of their visit. The assistant asks the questions; the rep speaks the answers; the AI maps responses to structured CRM fields and submits the completed survey.

For teams doing store visits, the module also includes a walk-and-talk mode. Instead of working through a checklist while standing in an aisle, reps speak their observations as they move through the store — shelf facings, stock levels, compliance, competitor activity — and the AI structures the input in real time.

## What's included

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [CRM survey sync](/customer/productivity/workflows/survey) | Pulls active marketing surveys from your CRM so reps always have the current version |
| [Voice-guided survey completion](/customer/productivity/workflows/survey) | Assistant asks the questions; rep speaks answers; AI maps responses to structured CRM fields |
| Survey briefings | Reps see which surveys are active for an account before arriving |
| Perfect store mode | Walk through a store and speak your observations — shelf facings, stock levels, compliance, competitor activity — captured and structured in real time as you move |
