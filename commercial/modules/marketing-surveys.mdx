Adds voice-guided survey completion and a walk-and-talk mode for store visits to Field Sales Fundamentals.

<Note>This module is in development. Contact your VoiceLine account manager for expected availability.</Note>

Marketing departments invest in surveys to understand market conditions, promotional execution, and customer sentiment — but the data rarely comes back. Reps deprioritise survey completion because it adds work on top of their core documentation, and there's no convenient way to fill them in during a visit.

For teams doing store visits, the problem is compounded. Auditing a store against brand standards — checking shelf facings, stock levels, planogram compliance, pricing, and competitor activity — can take up to an hour of standing in an aisle working through a checklist. The Marketing Surveys module solves both: marketing-created surveys get completed during the visit by voice, and for store audits, a dedicated walk-and-talk mode lets reps speak their observations as they move through the store. What previously took an hour takes a few minutes.

**What to measure**
- Survey completion rate across your rep population
- Time per survey compared to the manual baseline
