> **€900 / month** *(Beta)*

Extends the Market Analytics included in Field Sales Fundamentals with deeper drill-down pages, unlimited custom dashboards, and custom trackers.

## Why it exists

FSF Market Analytics gives leadership a real-time view of competitive presence, product sentiment, and industry trends. Market Analytics Pro goes further: instead of seeing aggregate numbers, you can drill into individual competitor profiles, industry profiles, and product profiles — and build custom dashboards tailored to the questions your business cares about most.

For companies where market intelligence is a strategic input — informing pricing decisions, rep training, product development, or key account strategy — the difference between a high-level overview and a structured, drillable dataset is significant. Custom trackers let you monitor specific signals (a competitor's new product line, a market shift in a key industry) systematically, not just reactively.

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Market Analytics Pro**. The base analytics features remain available after the trial period ends.</Note>

## What's included

Everything in FSF Market Analytics, plus:

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Dashboard sub-pages](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) | Drill into individual competitor, industry, or product profiles from the overview dashboard |
| Unlimited custom dashboards | Build as many tailored views as your team needs — no cap on dashboard count |
| Custom trackers | Monitor specific signals (a competitor, a market trend, a product) with a dedicated tracker |
