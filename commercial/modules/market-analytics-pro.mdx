<Note>Market Analytics Pro is not yet available for general activation. If you're interested in early access, speak to your VoiceLine solutions architect.</Note>

> **€900 / month**

Extends the Market Analytics included in Field Sales Fundamentals with deeper drill-down pages, unlimited custom dashboards, and custom trackers.

FSF Market Analytics gives you a real-time view of competitive presence, product sentiment, and industry trends. Market Analytics Pro goes further: instead of seeing aggregate numbers, you can drill into individual competitor profiles, industry profiles, and product profiles — and build custom dashboards tailored to the questions your business cares about most.

For companies where market intelligence is a strategic input — informing pricing decisions, rep training, product development, or key account strategy — the difference between a high-level overview and a structured, drillable dataset is significant. Custom trackers let you monitor specific signals (a competitor's new product line, a market shift in a key industry) systematically, not just reactively.

**What to measure**
- Competitive signals surfaced per week across the team (visible in the analytics dashboard)
- Distinct competitor or market insights used as inputs to commercial decisions per quarter

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Market Analytics Pro**. The base analytics features remain available after the trial period ends.</Note>

Everything in FSF Market Analytics, plus:

| Custom trackers *(Coming soon)* | Monitor specific signals (a competitor, a market trend, a product) with a dedicated tracker |
