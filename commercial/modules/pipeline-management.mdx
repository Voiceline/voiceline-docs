> **€400 / month**

Adds opportunity capture by voice and pipeline health tracking to Field Sales Fundamentals.

Deals get identified in the field — during a customer visit, on the way back from an account, or in a brief call between appointments. Without a fast way to record them, your reps lose the context by the time they're back at a desk, or skip creating the opportunity entirely. Pipeline data stays incomplete, forecasting is unreliable, and you have no visibility into what's actually being worked.

Pipeline Management makes opportunity capture a 30-second voice action. Your reps speak the deal details immediately after identifying them — the AI extracts the structured fields and writes directly to the CRM pipeline. No navigation, no manual field entry. Every rep capturing in real time means you see the full pipeline — not just the deals they remembered to log.

**What to measure**
- Opportunities captured per rep per month that would previously have been missed or logged late
- Pipeline coverage ratio before and after rollout (opportunities tracked vs. estimated deals worked)
