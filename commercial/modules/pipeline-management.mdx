Adds opportunity capture by voice and pipeline health tracking to Field Sales Fundamentals.

## Why it exists

Deals get identified in the field — during a customer visit, on the way back from an account, or in a brief call between appointments. Without a fast way to record them, reps either lose the context by the time they're back at a desk, or skip creating the opportunity entirely. Pipeline data stays incomplete, forecasting is unreliable, and managers have no visibility into what's actually being worked.

Pipeline Management solves this by making opportunity capture a 30-second voice action. Reps speak the deal details immediately after identifying them — the AI extracts the structured fields and writes directly to the CRM pipeline. No navigation, no manual field entry.

The value compounds with scale: every rep capturing opportunities in real time means leadership sees the full pipeline — not just the deals reps remembered to log.
