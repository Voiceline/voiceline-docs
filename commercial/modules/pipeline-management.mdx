> **€400 / month**

Adds opportunity capture by voice and pipeline health tracking to Field Sales Fundamentals.

Deals get identified in the field — during a customer visit, on the way back from an account, or in a brief call between appointments. Without a fast way to record them, context is lost by the time reps are back at a desk, and opportunities get logged late, incompletely, or not at all.

Pipeline Management adds opportunity capture and update by voice directly to the FSF workflow. Reps speak deal details immediately after identifying them — the AI extracts the structured fields and creates the CRM record on the spot. Existing opportunities can be updated the same way: stage, amount, close date — only the fields mentioned are changed.

## What's included

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Capture new opportunities by voice](/customer/productivity/workflows/opportunity) | Speak deal details immediately after a visit — AI extracts fields and creates the CRM record |
| [Update existing opportunities by voice](/customer/productivity/workflows/opportunity) | Change stage, amount, or close date hands-free — only the fields you mention are updated |
| [Opportunity status in visit briefings](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | See open deal status for each account in your pre-visit preparation |
| Pipeline health dashboard *(Coming soon)* | Track open deals, stage distribution, and team pipeline coverage in the web app |
