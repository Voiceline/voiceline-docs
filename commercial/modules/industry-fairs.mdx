Purpose-built module for capturing and tracking interactions at industry fairs.

<Note>Requires the [CRM Data Quality](/commercial/modules/customer-data) module and [Pipeline Management](/commercial/modules/pipeline-management) module.</Note>

At a busy trade fair, your reps speak to hundreds of people across multiple days. Without a dedicated capture tool, leads get scribbled on business cards, fair reports are written from memory days later, and campaign ROI is impossible to measure. The fair mode gives your team a fast, offline-capable capture flow — and you a live view of interactions, leads, and campaign performance during and after the fair.

## Pricing

Industry Fairs pricing has two components: a flat platform fee per workspace and a pre-purchased day-pass package.

**Platform fee**

> **€150 / month** — covers fair management, analytics, reports, lead tracking, and integration maintenance year-round

**Day-pass packages** — for external users without a VoiceLine licence

Day passes grant temporary fair access to people who are not licensed VoiceLine users — for example, colleagues from other departments, or temporary staff brought in specifically for the fair. A day pass covers one external person for one day. Existing VoiceLine users on your team do not require a day pass to participate in a fair.

A team of 10 licensed reps plus 5 temporary staff at a 3-day fair requires 15 day-passes.

Each day pass includes **400 credits**, enough to cover up to 100 lead captures per external user per day. These credits are separate from your workspace credit pool — fair activity from external users does not draw down your team's baseline.

Day-pass packages are purchased in advance. Contact your VoiceLine solutions architect to select the right package size.

## What's included

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| Quick-capture mode | Optimised recording flow for high-volume fair interactions — faster than a standard visit report |
| Full offline support | Record voice and capture images without connectivity; syncs automatically when back online |
| Customisable fair report questions | Configure report fields per campaign or event — different fairs can have different structures |
| Campaign tracking | Tag interactions with campaign identifiers to measure fair ROI and follow-up conversion |
| Live lead tracking | See leads captured per fair in real time during the event |
| Analytics and data export | Review fair performance metrics and export interaction data as CSV — filterable by date range |
