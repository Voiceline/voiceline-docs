Purpose-built module for capturing and tracking interactions at industry fairs.

<Note>Requires the [CRM Data Quality](/commercial/modules/customer-data) module and [Pipeline Management](/commercial/modules/pipeline-management) module.</Note>

At a busy trade fair, your reps speak to hundreds of people across multiple days. Without a dedicated capture tool, leads get scribbled on business cards, fair reports are written from memory days later, and campaign ROI is impossible to measure. The fair mode gives your team a fast, offline-capable capture flow — and you a live view of interactions, leads, and campaign performance during and after the fair.

## Pricing

Industry Fairs pricing has two components: a flat platform fee per workspace and a pre-purchased day-pass package covering the number of person-days at fairs.

A **day-pass** covers one person using VoiceLine at a fair for one day. A team of 10 attending a 3-day fair uses 30 day-passes.

| Feature | What it does |

| Quick-capture mode | Optimised recording flow for high-volume fair interactions — faster than a standard visit report |
