> **€900 / month** *(Beta)*

Extends the Sales Coaching included in Field Sales Fundamentals with unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution.

FSF includes one sales playbook — enough to run a single coaching initiative across your team. But most organisations need more. A product launch requires its own playbook. Key account reps need different guidance than territory reps. New hires need an onboarding track separate from experienced sellers.

Sales Coaching Pro removes the single-playbook ceiling. With unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution, you can run multiple simultaneous coaching initiatives — each targeted at the right reps based on role, territory, product focus, or any other segmentation. Every rep consistently coached according to their specific context drives better conversation quality, stronger objection handling, and more disciplined pipeline progression — across the whole team, not just a single initiative.

**What to measure**
- Playbook coverage rate: percentage of reps receiving active coaching across all running initiatives
- Number of simultaneous coaching initiatives running in parallel (product launches, segments, onboarding tracks)

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Sales Coaching Pro**. The single-playbook tier remains available after the trial period ends.</Note>
