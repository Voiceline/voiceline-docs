> **€900 / month** *(Beta)*

Extends the Sales Coaching included in Field Sales Fundamentals with unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution.

FSF includes one active playbook — sufficient for a single coaching initiative running across your whole team. Sales Coaching Pro removes that limit. You can run multiple playbooks simultaneously, each distributed only to the reps it applies to — by role, territory, product focus, or any other criteria. A product launch playbook goes to the relevant segment; a new hire onboarding track goes to recent joiners; key account guidance goes to the reps managing those accounts. Each rep receives coaching relevant to their context.

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Sales Coaching Pro**. The single-playbook tier remains available after the trial period ends.</Note>

## What's included

Everything in FSF Sales Coaching, plus:

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Unlimited playbooks](/customer/coaching/playbooks/builder) | Create separate playbooks for product launches, key accounts, new hires, seasonal campaigns — no ceiling |
| [Conditional target group distribution](/customer/coaching/playbooks/builder) | Deliver each playbook only to the reps who match specific criteria — role, territory, product focus, or custom fields |
