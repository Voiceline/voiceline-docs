Extends the Sales Coaching included in Field Sales Fundamentals with unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution.

## Why it exists

FSF includes one sales playbook — enough to run a single coaching initiative across your team. But most organisations need more. A product launch requires its own playbook. Key account reps need different guidance than territory reps. New hires need an onboarding track separate from experienced sellers. A seasonal campaign needs its own question set.

Sales Coaching Pro removes the single-playbook ceiling. With unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution, managers can run multiple simultaneous coaching initiatives — each targeted at the right reps based on role, territory, product focus, or any other segmentation. The coaching infrastructure scales with the organisation without adding administrative overhead.

The value is multiplicative: every rep consistently coached according to their specific context drives better conversation quality, stronger objection handling, and more disciplined pipeline progression — across the whole team, not just a single initiative.

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Sales Coaching Pro**. The single-playbook tier remains available after the trial period ends.</Note>
