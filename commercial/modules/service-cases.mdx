Adds service case creation and intelligent ticket routing to Field Sales Fundamentals.

<Note>This module is in development. Contact your VoiceLine account manager for expected availability.</Note>

When a field rep or service technician encounters a customer problem, the current process is slow: call inside sales, leave a message, send an email, or write it down and log it later. Context gets lost in transit, cases are logged late with incomplete detail, and routing to the right queue happens manually — or not at all.

Service Cases gives your field workers a direct path from incident to CRM case without any of that friction. Your rep speaks a case record on the spot — describing the issue, linking it to the customer and visit, selecting a priority, and routing it to the appropriate team queue — before leaving the car park. Inside sales receives a complete, structured case immediately, and the rep's visit report is automatically linked for full context.

**What to measure**
- Cases created from the field per month compared to the prior process
- Case log completeness: average fields captured per case compared to manually logged cases

## What's included

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Case creation by voice](/customer/productivity/workflows/case) | Describe the issue out loud — AI structures the record and creates it in your CRM before you leave |
| [Case updates from the field](/customer/productivity/workflows/case) | Add resolution notes, spare parts, or compliance observations by voice as work progresses |
| Intelligent ticket routing | Assign cases to a specific person or a team queue — matching how CRM service workflows operate |
| Open case status in visit briefings | See outstanding cases for an account as part of pre-visit preparation |
| Visit report linking | Cases are automatically connected to the visit report that triggered them for full context |
