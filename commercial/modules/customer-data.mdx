> **€250 / month**

Adds voice-first creation and update of lead, contact, and account master data to Field Sales Fundamentals, with AI-assisted deduplication that navigates across all three object types.

Clean master data is the foundation of reliable sales reporting — and it erodes constantly in the field. New contacts don't get created because reps have no convenient way to add them on the go. Account records go stale because corrections require desktop access. Duplicate entries accumulate because there's no check at the point of entry.

The CRM Data Quality module makes master data maintenance part of the natural field workflow. Your reps create and update lead, contact, and account records by voice, directly after a visit. What makes this different from a simple creation tool is the AI deduplication logic: it searches across all three object types before a rep commits a record. If a rep tries to create a new lead, VoiceLine might surface an existing contact — and guide the rep to update that contact's details instead. The right action, on the right record.

**What to measure**
- Leads, contacts, and accounts created or updated from the field per month
- Duplicate records prevented via AI detection
