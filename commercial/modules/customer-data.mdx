> **€250 / month**

Adds voice-first creation and update of lead, contact, and account master data to Field Sales Fundamentals, with AI-assisted deduplication that navigates across all three object types.

Keeping CRM master data accurate requires corrections to happen at the right moment — in the field, while the interaction is still fresh. The CRM Data Quality module adds voice-first creation and update of lead, contact, and account records to the FSF workflow, so reps can act on new information immediately after a visit rather than logging it later from a desk.

Before a record is created, AI deduplication searches across all three object types. If a rep tries to create a new lead but a matching contact already exists, VoiceLine surfaces it and guides the rep to update that record instead — preventing duplicate entries at the point of creation.
