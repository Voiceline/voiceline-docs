> **€250 / month**

Adds account and contact data quality tools, lead creation, and a business card scanner to Field Sales Fundamentals.

## Why it exists

CRM data degrades silently. Contacts change jobs, accounts merge, and new leads never make it in because reps have no convenient way to add them from the field. Sales and marketing teams make decisions based on data they suspect is wrong but can't easily fix.

The Customer Data module closes this loop. When a rep meets someone new at a customer visit, they can create a contact or lead instantly by voice or by scanning a business card. When account information is outdated, they update it from their phone before leaving the car park. VoiceLine checks for duplicates automatically — surfacing likely matches before the rep commits a record — so the CRM stays clean without dedicated data hygiene work.
