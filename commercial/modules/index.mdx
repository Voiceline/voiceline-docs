Modules extend your VoiceLine platform with additional workflows, capabilities, and use cases beyond what Field Sales Fundamentals covers. Each module is activated individually once your FSF rollout is complete.

Every module is priced as a flat monthly fee per workspace — there are no per-user or scaling costs. Activating a module makes it available to your entire team for that fixed fee, regardless of how many reps use it. The usage that follows — CRM records created, workflows triggered, outputs produced — is measured and billed through [Credits](/commercial/credits), the same way FSF usage is.

<Note>All modules require [Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals/index) to be fully rolled out before activation. Modules are activated one at a time.</Note>
