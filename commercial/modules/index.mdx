Add-on modules that extend Field Sales Fundamentals with additional capabilities and use cases.

<Note>All modules require [Field Sales Fundamentals](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals) to be fully rolled out before activation.</Note>

## Pro tier upgrades

| Module | Price | What it adds |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| [Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro) | €900 / month | Sub-pages, custom dashboards, custom trackers |
| [Sales Coaching Pro](/commercial/modules/sales-coaching-pro) | €900 / month | Unlimited playbooks, conditional target groups |

| Module | Price | What it adds |
