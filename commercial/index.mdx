VoiceLine uses a modular pricing model. You start with **Field Sales Fundamentals** — a mandatory base package — and add capabilities through modules as your rollout grows. There is no all-in-one tier; you buy exactly the capabilities you need on top of a shared foundation.

## How your setup is priced

VoiceLine pricing is built around what you actually deploy — not a fixed tier.

**Field Sales Fundamentals — per user, per month**
Your starting point. Every user needs this: it covers voice documentation, visit briefings, market analytics, and coaching. At **€49 / user / month**, this is the only fee that scales with your team size.

**Platform package — one per workspace**
Determines which languages, authentication methods, and user management controls are available across your whole organisation. Pick Professional (€900 / month) or Enterprise (€2,490 / month) — this doesn't change as your team grows.

**Modules — add capabilities as you need them**
Each module unlocks a specific use case across your whole workspace for a flat monthly fee, regardless of how many users activate it. You activate modules one at a time, after FSF is adopted. Prices range from €150 to €900 / month.

**Credits — based on how much AI processing you use**
FSF includes 500 credits / user / month pooled across your workspace. Credits are consumed by AI-powered actions like processing visit reports, extracting analytics, and creating CRM records. If you exceed your included allowance, you buy additional packages from €105.

<Note>Module and Platform fees are flat per workspace and do not scale with seat count. Only FSF is per-user. Credits scale with actual product usage.</Note>

## Implementation fees

Custom configuration work is billed separately at **€1,300 per person day**. This applies to non-standard setups — VPN access, custom CRM object mappings, or integrations outside the standard connector library. Standard implementations are included in the onboarding process.

50 field reps on Professional with two modules and a custom CRM object mapping (2 implementation days):

| Item | Calculation | Cost |
|------|------------|------|
| Field Sales Fundamentals | 50 users × €49 | €2,450 / month |
| Platform Professional | Flat fee | €900 / month |
| Pipeline Management | Flat fee | €400 / month |
| CRM Data Quality | Flat fee | €250 / month |
| **Recurring total** | | **€4,000 / month** |
| Custom CRM object mapping | 2 days × €1,300 | €2,600 one-off |

Credits are included in FSF (50 × 500 = 25,000 credits / month pooled). Additional usage is billed on top.
