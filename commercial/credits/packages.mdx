When your workspace exceeds the monthly baseline included in FSF, you can purchase additional credit packages. Packages add directly to the workspace pool and activate immediately.

Packages are calculated monthly. Unused credits from a given month do not roll over — plan your package size based on your team's expected monthly output volume.

| Package | Additional credits | Per credit | Total |
|---------|------------------|-----------|-------|
| Starter | 1,500 | €0.045 | €67.50 |
| Basic | 5,000 | €0.040 | €200 |
| Standard | 15,000 | €0.035 | €525 |
| Pro | 50,000 | €0.032 | €1,600 |
| Enterprise | 200,000 | €0.030 | €6,000 |

## How to purchase a package

Credit packages are not available for self-service purchase in the app. To add credits to your workspace, contact your VoiceLine solutions architect. They will advise on the right package size based on your team's usage profile and arrange the purchase.
