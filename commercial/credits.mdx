VoiceLine Credits are the usage-based component of VoiceLine pricing. They measure what the product produces — CRM records created, updated, and insights extracted — rather than how long a rep spends interacting with the assistant.

## How credits work

Every VoiceLine workspace has a credit pool. The pool is shared across all users in the workspace. When VoiceLine produces an output — a visit report, an opportunity, a task — the corresponding credits are drawn from the pool.

**Credits are consumed by outputs, not inputs.** The interaction mode (memo, conversation, short voice command, app interaction) does not affect how many credits are used. What matters is what lands in the CRM.

## Credit baseline (included in FSF)

Field Sales Fundamentals includes a credit baseline of **150 credits per active user per month**, pooled at the workspace level.

| Team size | Monthly baseline |
|-----------|-----------------|
| 15 users | 2,250 credits |
| 30 users | 4,500 credits |
| 100 users | 15,000 credits |
| 300 users | 45,000 credits |

The baseline grows automatically as users are added to the workspace. It is recalculated monthly based on active user count.

## What consumes credits

| Output | Credits | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Opportunity / lead / case created | 3 | Directly pipeline-linked; highest business impact |
| Visit report / interaction record created | 2 | Core FSF output; also triggers analytics processing |
| Account or contact record updated | 2 | Field-sourced CRM data quality improvement |
| Task / reminder / email draft created | 1 | Utility outputs; high volume but lower impact |
| Analytics insight extracted | 1 | Background processing from recordings (see note below) |

A single interaction can produce multiple outputs — all are counted. A memo that creates a visit report and surfaces 3 competitive signals = 5 credits total. A short "create opportunity" command = 3 credits.

<Note>Analytics insight credits apply to customers with the [Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro) module. The exact definition of one extractable insight is pending product confirmation and will be updated here when finalised.</Note>

## When the pool runs out

If the monthly baseline is exhausted, VoiceLine continues operating by drawing from any purchased credit packages. If no package balance is available, outputs that would exceed the pool are queued — workspace admins receive a notification and can purchase a package to release them.

Workspace admins can monitor credit consumption and remaining balance in **Settings → Billing → Credits**.

## Credit packages

Packages add to the workspace pool on top of the monthly baseline. Packages are purchased in advance and valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Unused credits carry over within the validity window — they are not forfeited at the end of each month.

| Package | Additional credits | Per credit | Total |
|---------|------------------|-----------|-------|
| Starter | 1,500 | €0.07 | €105 |
| Basic | 5,000 | €0.06 | €300 |
| Standard | 15,000 | €0.05 | €750 |
| Pro | 50,000 | €0.04 | €2,000 |
| Enterprise | 200,000 | €0.03 | €6,000 |

<Note>Per-credit rates are pending Finance confirmation and will be published here once validated. Contact your VoiceLine account manager for current pricing.</Note>

## Purchasing a package

Workspace admins can purchase credit packages directly from **Settings → Billing → Credits → Buy credits**. Packages activate immediately and the balance is added to the workspace pool.

For Enterprise package purchases or multi-package agreements, contact your VoiceLine account manager.
