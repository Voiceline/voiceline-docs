Reps currently spend 1–2 hours per day on documentation — after hours, at the end of the week, or not at all. The productivity features in FSF replace that overhead with a voice-first workflow: speak after each visit, and the AI handles the rest. Every output syncs to the CRM in real time.

## AI Sales Assistant

The assistant is the core interface for reps in the field. It handles voice interactions, guides onboarding, and enables hands-free use while driving.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Mobile app setup](/customer/productivity/assistant) | Configure your assistant's voice, language, and preferences in the app |
| AI-led onboarding call | Walks you through your first recordings with a guided call |
| Hands-free calling while driving | Talk to your assistant and capture activities without stopping the car |
| 500 credits / user / month | Baseline usage allowance per user, pooled at workspace level — see [Credits](/commercial/credits) |

→ [Assistant docs](/customer/productivity/assistant)

## Productivity Workflows

Every core field activity is captured by voice and synced to the CRM in real time. Your reps document more completely, faster, and without interrupting the flow of their day.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Visit Report](/customer/productivity/workflows/visit-report) | Log customer visits by voice — AI extracts structured fields and syncs to CRM |
| [Call Log](/customer/productivity/workflows/call-log) | Document phone calls by speaking a summary immediately after hanging up |
| [Task](/customer/productivity/workflows/task) | Create and assign follow-up tasks by voice, linked to the account or visit |
| [Reminder](/customer/productivity/workflows/reminder) | Set personal reminders hands-free while still in the field |
| [Meeting](/customer/productivity/workflows/meeting) | Create and confirm customer meetings by voice, synced to calendar and CRM |
| [Email](/customer/productivity/workflows/email) | Dictate emails to customers — AI drafts, formats, and sends on your behalf |

→ [Workflows overview](/customer/productivity/workflows/index)

## Visit Briefing

Before each visit, reps receive an AI-generated briefing covering past interactions, open action points, and key context — arriving prepared instead of relying on memory.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Action points and to-dos](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | AI summarises open tasks and priorities from past visit data before you leave |
| [Printable briefing report](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | Download a formatted brief to review before the visit |
| [Customer activity timeline](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | See all recorded interactions with the account in chronological order |
| [Briefing call while driving](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | Ask your assistant follow-up questions hands-free on the way to the visit |
