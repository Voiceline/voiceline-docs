Effective coaching requires visibility into what's actually happening in the field. FSF gives managers a live view of team activity, documentation quality, and playbook coverage — built from real visit data, not self-reported numbers.

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Sales Coaching Pro**. The Pro tier unlocks unlimited playbooks and conditional target group distribution. See [Modules → Sales Coaching Pro](/commercial/modules/sales-coaching-pro).</Note>

## Activity Tracking *(Beta)*

See how active your team is, how completely they document, and which reps are building strong habits versus falling behind.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Team productivity tracking](/customer/coaching/team-activity) | Monitor visit frequency, documentation rate, and activity trends per rep |
| [Marketfeedback Score](/customer/coaching/team-activity) | Score the depth and completeness of visit reports across the team |
| [Personal coaching profiles](/customer/coaching/team-activity) | AI-generated rep-level profiles highlighting strengths and development areas |

→ [Team Activity docs](/customer/coaching/team-activity)

## Sales Playbooks *(Beta)*

Define which topics and questions your reps should cover in customer meetings. Playbooks appear automatically during recordings — a lightweight guide that shapes field execution without adding mandatory steps.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Default playbook](/customer/coaching/playbooks/index) | Define a question set that reps are briefed and debriefed before and after visits |
| [Playbook builder](/customer/coaching/playbooks/builder) | Create and distribute playbooks with questions and target audiences |
| [Visit briefing and debriefing integration](/customer/productivity/visit-briefing) | Playbook topics surface in pre-visit preparation and post-visit review |
| [Coverage and response analytics](/customer/coaching/playbooks/analytics) | Track how consistently reps cover each question across visits |

→ [Playbooks docs](/customer/coaching/playbooks/index)

## Visit Analytics *(Beta)*

An AI-curated feed of field visits with quality scoring and topic analysis. Spot patterns across your whole team without listening to individual recordings.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [AI-curated visit feed](/customer/coaching/visit-analytics) | Chronological overview of recent visits, surfacing the most relevant ones |
| [Sales progression analysis](/customer/coaching/visit-analytics) | Highlights strengths and weaknesses in how reps move deals forward |
| [Topic frequency analysis](/customer/coaching/visit-analytics) | Shows which subjects come up most — and least — across your team |
| [Objection handling tracking](/customer/coaching/visit-analytics) | Measures how well reps identify and respond to customer objections |
