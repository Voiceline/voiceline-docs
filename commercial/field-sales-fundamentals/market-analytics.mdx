Every field conversation your reps have contains competitive signals, product feedback, and industry intelligence. FSF captures all of it automatically — no extra fields to fill in, no separate survey process. Your team's visits become a continuously updated picture of the market.

<Note>FSF includes a **1-month free trial of Market Analytics Pro**. The Pro tier unlocks sub-pages per dashboard, unlimited custom dashboards, and custom trackers. See [Modules → Market Analytics Pro](/commercial/modules/market-analytics-pro).</Note>

## Market Data Lake

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|

| [Market movement recognition](/customer/analytics/data-lake) | Groups signals into trends across accounts, competitors, and industries |

## Competitor Analytics *(Beta)*

Aggregates competitor mentions across all your reps' recordings to show where competitors are winning and where they're vulnerable — without asking anyone to fill in extra fields.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| [Competitive field movement tracking](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) | Detects competitor mentions automatically from visit recordings |
| [Competitor presence leaderboard](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) | Ranks competitors by mention frequency across your team's visits |
| [Strength and weakness analysis](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) | Scores competitors across price, quality, service, and delivery dimensions |
| [Market share dynamics](/customer/analytics/competitor-analytics) | Tracks which accounts are open to switching supplier |
