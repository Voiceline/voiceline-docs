Field Sales Fundamentals is the base package every VoiceLine customer starts with. It is required for all users before any modules can be activated.

> **€49 per user / month** — the only per-user fee in VoiceLine pricing.

## Three pillars, one foundation

FSF bundles three capability areas into a single licence:

**[Frontline Productivity](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals/productivity)**
Voice-first documentation for every core field activity — visit reports, call logs, tasks, meetings, and emails — plus AI-generated visit briefings. Reps spend their time selling, not on admin.

**[Market Analytics](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals/market-analytics)**
Every field conversation is automatically mined for competitive signals, product feedback, and industry trends. Your team's visits become a continuously updated market intelligence feed without any extra effort.

**[Sales Coaching](/commercial/field-sales-fundamentals/sales-coaching)**
Live visibility into team activity, documentation quality, and playbook coverage. Coach on real field data instead of self-reported numbers.

Every user on an FSF licence gets access to all three areas from day one.

## Credits — usage is included

FSF includes **500 credits per user per month**, pooled across the whole workspace. Credits measure what VoiceLine produces — visit reports filed, CRM records created, insights extracted — not how long reps spend talking to the assistant.

For most teams, the included baseline is enough for normal daily use. As your rollout matures and reps use VoiceLine more intensively — or as you activate modules that generate additional outputs — you can purchase additional credit packages on demand.
